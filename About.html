About Us

Food Find is driven by a desire to create a more equitable and sustainable food system. We see a troubling disconnect: on one hand, restaurants grapple with expiring food nearing its sell-by date, perfectly good to eat but destined for the landfill. On the other hand, many individuals and families struggle with food insecurity. Food Find steps in as a bridge, connecting these two realities. We partner with restaurants to collect their surplus food – meals, produce, and dairy products – that would otherwise go to waste.

This food is carefully inspected, packaged, and delivered to a network of charitable organizations, including food banks, shelters, and community kitchens. Through these partnerships, Food Find ensures that nutritious, delicious meals reach those who need them most. By rescuing food and diverting it from landfills, we contribute to a more responsible food system and nourish our communities, one delicious bite at a time.
